A word on this website...

My name is Gabor, and I have ADHD. I created this website as a project to help people who have depression, impaired executive functioning or generally have a hard time dealing with cooking and being in the kitchen just makes them feel overwhelmed.

I think the main theme captures a real-world problem and it has a targeted audience who could really benefit from a website like this. I really enjoyed making and designing this project with the intention of creating a tool I would use.
